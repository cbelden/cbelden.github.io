What I do.

MTS Systems Corporation

Automated UI Testing: C#

This past summer I interned as a software test engineer at MTS Systems in Eden Prairie, MN. MTS produces testing devices for a variety of fields and many of these devices are controlled by a Windows application, TestSuite.

I was a member of the automated test team that worked with TestSuite, and I focused on building a coded-UI automated testing framework.

I also participated in the annual Hackathon and built a web-based phone service for customer feedback submissions. The service allowed customers to query the status of existing feedback items, create new items, and receive updates: all over the phone.

Gentex Corporation

Firmware Dev: C

Integration Testing: Ruby

The summer of 2012 I interned as a software engineer at Gentex, a global car mirror manufacturer in Zeeland, Michigan. I wrote diagnostic commands to communicate and send commands to the mirror.

In addition, I also wrote integration tests to ensure the car mirror's core features were behaving appropriately. I tested features like automatic dimming and displaying accurate compass and GPS information.
